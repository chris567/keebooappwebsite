Company Overview

Keeboo App LLC develops and operates Keeboo, a mobile application focused on sharing food content and practical home cooking recipes. The application provides a streamlined format for posting ingredients, preparation steps, and related media in a structured and accessible way.

Keeboo is designed to prioritize clarity, usability, and everyday cooking over professionally produced or influencer-driven content.
